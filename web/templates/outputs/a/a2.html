  A2: Personas

Ambitious Amber

“I have a cool app idea! I just need someone to code it.”

Biography

Amber grew up in Palo Alto, California. A few years ago, she decided to move and live near the Silicon Valley: a booming epicenter of many successful high-technology companies such as Apple, Google and Facebook. It is always her dream to work on one of these companies. She is not that invested in developing apps, but she does use them a lot. Undoubtedly, she is a tech enthusiast herself! She loves using her favorite apps on her phone in her day-to-day life. She likes posting social media statuses every time. Before she eats a hearty meal, she takes a photo of it and posts it on Instagram. She always fears of missing out. Every second of her life is surely documented online. She is a person that you would always see online 24/7. Every now and then, she likes to keep in-touch with recent news from various tech companies. She is always excited for the next app that will hit the market. She is also wondering if her app ideas can see the light of day.

Demographics
  • Age: 26
  • Location: Palo Alto, California
  • Education: Arts
  • Occupation: Self-employed
Traits
  • Jolly, extroverted, dense
Goals
  • To be able to make her app ideas come to life
  • To be able to establish a business that is centered on delivering software products
  • To see if her app ideas are now taken by browsing portfolio sites
Frustrations
  • Terrible with programming
  • Unfamiliar with technical design of apps
  • Too much time spent with her apps
Favorite Apps
  • Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Uber, Cashapp and Snapchat
Busy Bary

“Time is money. No time to relax. Relaxation is a scam, people!”

Biography

Bary is all about business. He is a territory channel manager on Microsoft. He is always busy and looking for ways to expand his company’s reach. Occasionally, he is always on the lookout for potential talent that could benefit his company. He enjoys hosting hackathons and participating as a judge among these competitions. Bary may be a serious guy, but he has a big heart for the youth. He likes to listen to startup ideas and from the youth. With this, he likes inspiring them . He believes that the youth are society’s hope and salvation. You can occasionally see him give calling cards to those that have impressed him. Or perhaps, you can see him answering a phone call while enjoying his lunch. Before going to sleep, he likes checking his stock trading app. He does not seem to relax. He is always busy after all.

Demographics
  • Age: 42
  • Location: Redmond, Washington
  • Education: Business Relations
  • Occupation: Territory Channel Manager
Traits
  • Serious, business-minded, time-bound
Goals
  • To find talent that could benefit his company
  • To seek potential startup ideas that he could invest in
  • To search for impressive portfolios and candidates
Frustrations
  • Light colors and high contrast color schemes make him irritated
  • Other portfolio sites are not effective enough in showcasing content and selling themselves
  • Non-responsive design makes him repel the app completely
Favorite Apps
  • Outlook, TD Ameritrade, LinkedIn, Teams, OneNote, Skype, Word
Tech Tim

“Wow! I wonder what runs this website?”

Biography

Tim likes to tinker with stuff. Tim is a web developer by profession. Currently, he is doing freelance work on Fiverr.com. He likes to scour the internet for project ideas. He likes looking for design inspiration from other websites and online portfolios. He likes building a portfolio of himself also. He is a well-known person in the technology field. He even has a Twitter account called @techtimtinkertips; where he gives technical advice and project showcases in what he is currently working on. He likes learning a lot and sharing what he knows. He is always the guy who likes giving free coupon codes of his Udemy courses online. Knowing this, he is a practitioner by heart as well. He produces educational online content about HTML, CSS and JavaScript development for beginners.

Demographics
  • Age: 37
  • Location: Springfield, Massachusetts
  • Education: Information Technology
  • Occupation: Web Developer
Traits
  • Curious, enthusiastic, tech savvy
Goals
  • To seek project ideas that he could build and challenge himself with
  • To find inspiration in how to layout and improve his own portfolio
  • To potentially collaborate with fellow developers online
Frustrations
  • Projects showcased in portfolio sites do not reveal much information to satisfy his curiosity
  • Slow loading times make him impatient
  • He occasionally cannot contact developers from their portfolio sites
Favorite Apps
  • Twitter, GitHub, Heroku, Digital Ocean, AWS, Udemy
